SEO for Contractors

What Exactly is Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and How Can it Help You Get More Phone Calls?

SEO is the start to getting an online presence, but that can mean many things.

Bottom Line: you want more high quality calls coming in to your business.

You want to make the PHONE RING, right?

Free Consultation

Let's start with Google

Let's start with Google

Where do you go when you want to find a local service?

97% of us pull out our phones and search Google.

Let's break this down:

1. You search Google

2. You scroll down to the Maps section

3. You browse some reviews

4. You skim a few of the top websites

5. You call 2 or 3 businesses to get pricing and availability

That's exactly what your customers do.

So, doesn't it make sense that if you want more potential customers you show up on the first page of the Google search results?

That, my friend, is called SEO or Search Engine Optimization--and that is what we have helped hundreds of service businesses do over the past 12 years.

SEO is hands down THE MOST powerful way to make your phone ring because people are searching you out and need something from you.

That means they are ready to spend money with you... Now!

How many calls and potential customers will you get from SEO?

Great Question!

Like most answers it depends on your unique situation.

There are so many factors that contribute to your call count. We have clients that get 5 calls per day from SEO. We also have clients that get 5 calls per week.

Let's break down what will determine your calls:

How long you have been doing SEO?

What kind of competition are you up against?

Does your website work on mobile devices?

Is your website designed to get calls?

What city or service area do you work in?

Are in you the busy season or slow season?

How many people are searching for your specific services?

What quality of customers can you expect from having a strong online presence?

Keep in mind that SEO is not just about "getting calls", it's also about getting high quality calls. You know that not all customers are equal. There are good customers and bad customers.

With a solid SEO program you are building a powerful online presence and reputation that you are THE BEST in town. This means repeat and referral work. It can snowball into a machine that keeps your schedule full with nothing but high quality jobs and prospective customers.

We have found that other marketing options just don't measure up when it comes to quality. That is why specialize in SEO strategy and don't pretend be a one-stop shop for everything.

Big West Marketing is the best in the business because we focus and do one thing very well. We create contractor SEO campaigns to rank for search terms and relevant keywords.

What are the ranking factors that get your business to the top of Google?

What you really need to understand is that Google's goal is to give people online the highest quality business in town.

So, if you do quality work and have a good reputation then you are already on your way.

There is no single ranking factor that will make all the difference.

There are no secret tricks or strategies, just good business practices.

There are no secret tricks or strategies, just good business practices.

Some of the most important ranking factors you need to think about are:

A good website with lots of helpful content marketing. Click here to learn more.

A Google My Business listing that is filled out completely and accurately with pictures.

Accurate information across all online websites and directories (Yelp, Facebook, etc.)

Links (aka backlinks) coming from other reputable websites. Click here to learn more.

Steady flow of customer reviews and social proof on your Google My Business page.

A Google Analytics account to keep track of progress

How long does it take for SEO to start working?

This is where many service business owners get stuck.

You want calls NOW. We get it.

Calls are the lifeblood of any successful service business.

But, for SEO to work you must be in it for the long-haul. This is for the serious open-minded business owners with vision. It can take anywhere from 3 to 6 to 12 months depending on your current situation and the competition you are up against.

If you are in desperation mode and need jobs coming in tomorrow or your family wont eat then SEO is not for you. We recommend getting out with flyers, door hangers, networking groups, etc. Or if you have an advertising budget of $1000 per month or more, you can always run paid ads on Google or Facebook. Just be prepared for lower quality clients that are price-shopping. You can make it work, but it won't be cheap and it's a temporary solution.

What is included in the monthly SEO program with us?

We conduct an audit of your SEO campaign. You will have access to monthly reports to keep a pulse on your business's progress up the ranks.

We review the ranking of your competitors and report to Google any listings that blatantly violate Google's terms of service and explain the actions steps that will taken.

Your website will receive a highly targeted backlink every other month over a period of 12 months for a total of 6 backlinks from our exclusive backlink network. The websites in our backlink network are carefully selected, designed and maintained based on related industry articles and authority with Google. This must be implemented slowly and naturally over time as not to trigger Google penalties that can result from too-many too-fast. This is one of the many exclusive SEO tactics provided at no extra cost.

In the SEO report we provide a detailed analysis of keywords and rankings for services and cities/areas.

Your website will be monitored for possible “blacklist” and/or penalty issues and recovery techniques will be implemented if necessary.

Design changes and updates to your website at no extra charge.

You will receive monthly video and article updates as changes occur so you are always in-the-know.

Your website will be tweaked and changed as new Google updates are implemented.

Security updates to the website to protect and prevent malicious attacks.

We ensure your Google My Business page and directory listings are up to date and comply with Google's ever-changing guidelines.

We note changes in rankings in Google Maps and Google's organic search results.

We review SERanking (our daily rank-tracking and backlink-tracking tool) to look for major changes, view real-time rankings, and identify patterns, which helps determine our next course of action.

Our marketing team also double-checks notes from past emails and phone conversations whenever we're communicating with you
